League Season 1 — Capture the Flag

Enter your bot in the next AutoNateAI Screeps League

Win condition

Capture the Flag (v1, borrowed from Screeps: Arena's simplest arena). Each side has one flag that cannot be destroyed or moved. First creep to touch the enemy's flag wins instantly. If time expires with neither flag touched, the match is a draw. No economy scoring, no judgment calls — just a position check. Future seasons can layer in economy (Spawn and Swamp style) or area-control scoring (Collect and Control style) once there's real gameplay data.
