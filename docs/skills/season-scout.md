# Skill: Season scout

**Purpose:** identify Roblox GameLab quest opportunities that can make money
quickly while still fitting the long-term organization workflow.

## Inputs

- Airtable Market Matrix OS records.
- Roblox trend, genre, and monetization notes.
- YouTube, Discord, Twitch, TikTok, or community signals.
- A target buyer or player segment.

## Output

A `seasonOpportunity` entry with:

- `id`
- `title`
- `engine`: `trend-sniper`, `organization`, `education`, or `marketplace`
- `audience`
- `buyer`
- `monetization`
- `evidence`
- `buildWindowDays`
- `risk`

## Method

1. Score opportunities by player demand, speed to playable, monetization fit,
   community access, and reuse in the organization workflow.
2. Prefer concepts that can be expressed as action loops, not lecture content.
3. Capture evidence links or Airtable record IDs before writing quest content.
4. Promote only the strongest opportunities into the quest composer.
